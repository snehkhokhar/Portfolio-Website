About this website

A portfolio website is a digital space that showcases your work, skills, and experiences. It can be a website, blog, or video channel. The purpose of a portfolio website is to:

Display your work

Showcase a selection of your best work, including class projects, design samples, or other creative projects.

Provide information

Include basic information about yourself, such as your resume, qualifications, and contact information.

Get clients

Help you land your next job by sharing your work with potential employers, collaborators, or the press.

Here are some tips for creating a portfolio website:

Include context

Provide details about your projects, such as who they were for and why they were needed.

Balance text and visuals

Don't overwhelm the reader with too much text.

Include your contact information

Make it easy for visitors to get in touch with you by including your contact information on multiple pages.

Show a few of your best pieces

It's better to include fewer, higher quality examples than many small, incomplete pieces.
